Quiz 6 · Explain the pattern (model answers)

1. Quiero — Stem-changing e→ie (querer). The stem 'quer' becomes 'quier' inside the boot.

2. Somos — Completely irregular (ser). It has no pattern — memorized: soy, eres, es, somos, son.

3. Traigo — Yo-form irregular (traer). Only the yo form adds -igo; the rest are regular.

4. Podemos — The stem does NOT change because nosotros sits OUTSIDE the boot — so o→ue doesn't apply.

5. Vienes — Combination verb (venir): the boot gives e→ie (vienes); the yo form is separately irregular (vengo).

6. Hago — Yo-form irregular (hacer): the yo form grows a g. The other forms (haces, hace…) are regular.

Bonus · tener across time

Present: tengo, tienes, tiene, tenemos, tenéis, tienen.  ·  Preterite (yo): tuve.  ·  Imperfect (yo): tenía.  ·  Future (yo): tendré.
